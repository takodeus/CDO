Your systems are

The enterprise AI transition in real estate is not a technology problem. It is a governance, memory, and organizational redesign problem. The firms deploying agents are discovering that the models work. What fails is everything around the model: the definitions feeding it were never agreed upon, the people reviewing its outputs were never redesigned for that role, and when something goes wrong, no one can trace why.

The firms that pull ahead won't have the most data or the fastest models. They will have systems that remember what things meant when decisions were made, and organizations designed to govern that meaning as it changes.
